My services

Study of the feasibility of the clients' wishes regarding building constructions

During this phase, i discuss with the customer about his wishes and budget.
I make also a visit in the area where the future building about whom i 've discusssed with the client will be built

Design of architectural plans

During this phase, i design an architectural plan based on the client’s needs.
During this phase, i consider any proposals from the client.
I analyze these proposals in order to verify their feasibility.

Planification

During this phase,
I use the components of the project management
plan to develop a plan to follow during the execution of the work. During this step, i create also diagrams to determine the order of precedence between the different project activities.
This allows the client to be regularly informed about the resources
that must be made available at each stage of the project .

Execution

During this phase, i make sure that what is being carried out on the site complies
with the architectural plan that i have developed.
I offer a service tailored to the client’s needs thanks to agile project management.
